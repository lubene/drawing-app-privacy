Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2025

Simple Version: We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

Introduction

Storybook Canvas is a drawing and creative canvas application designed for children and adults. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Our app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track user behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • Connect to the internet for data transmission

Data Storage

All drawings, artwork, and related content you create in Storybook Canvas are stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Remains entirely on your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by you through the app or by uninstalling the app
  • Is not backed up by us (you may back it up through your device's backup system)

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage: To save and load your drawings on your device

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate passwords and security settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all content created in the app, which you can delete at any time directly from your device.
